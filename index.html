Why Universal Orlando Resort?

Adventure for Families

Universal Orlando Resort is a thrilling destination that offers extraordinary adventures for families of all ages. The resort features four distinct theme parks, each with unique attractions that appeal to different interests and age groups.
At Universal Studios Florida, families can step into the worlds of their favorite movies and TV shows. The Wizarding World of Harry Potter - Diagon Alley lets you explore iconic locations from the beloved series, while Transformers: The Ride-3D and Despicable Me Minion Mayhem offer high-tech interactive experiences.
Islands of Adventure delivers more intense thrills with Marvel Super Hero Island and Jurassic Park attractions. The Wizarding World of Harry Potter - Hogsmeade connects to Diagon Alley via the Hogwarts Express, creating an immersive magical experience across both parks.
Volcano Bay water theme park provides a perfect way to cool off with innovative water slides, a relaxing lazy river, and a massive wave pool beneath the towering volcano, Krakatau.
Beyond the parks, CityWalk offers family-friendly dining, shopping, and entertainment options.
The resort hotels cater to different budgets and preferences, with themed accommodations like the retro Cabana Bay Beach Resort and the Italian-inspired Loews Portofino Bay Hotel.
What makes Universal Orlando particularly special for families is its ability to entertain across generations. While there are gentle attractions for younger children, there are also pulse-pounding roller coasters and sophisticated entertainment for teens and adults. Interactive elements throughout the parks, like wands that cast spells in the Wizarding World, create magical moments for the whole family to enjoy together. Whether you're seeking thrilling rides, immersive themed environments, or refreshing water attractions, Universal Orlando Resort delivers a comprehensive family adventure that creates lasting memories.

Why Universal Orlando Resort?

Adventure for Adults

Universal Orlando Resort offers an exceptional experience tailored for adults seeking thrills, sophistication, and entertainment.
The parks transform into a playground for grown-ups with high-intensity attractions like the Jurassic World VelociCoaster and Hollywood Rip Ride Rockit coaster, which feature inversions and speeds that might be too intense for younger visitors. Hagrid's Magical Creatures Motorbike Adventure combines storytelling with adrenaline-pumping moments that adults particularly appreciate. Universal's immersive themed areas shine differently through an adult lens.
The Wizarding World of Harry Potter appeals to the generation that grew up with the books, offering authentic butterbeer and specialty cocktails at the Hog's Head pub. Springfield, home of The Simpsons, features adult humor, Duff Gardens, and Moe's Tavern where you can enjoy a Duff beer.
CityWalk transforms after dark with sophisticated dining options like Toothsome Chocolate Emporium & Savory Feast Kitchen and NBC Sports Grill & Brew, plus entertainment venues including Rising Star (karaoke with a live band) and nightclubs that come alive in the evening hours.
The resort's premier hotels offer adult-oriented amenities worth experiencing. Strong Water Tavern at Loews Sapphire Falls Resort features over 75 rums, while the Pool Bars at Hard Rock Hotel host occasional live music. Many hotels feature luxurious spas perfect for post-park relaxation. Adults can maximize their experience with Express Passes to bypass regular lines or take advantage of the "single rider" options available at most major attractions to significantly reduce wait times.
Whether you're planning a couples getaway, a friends trip, or solo adventure, Universal Orlando provides a perfect blend of nostalgic references, adult beverages, sophisticated dining, and heart-pounding thrills designed to satisfy the most discerning grown-up theme park enthusiasts.

Favorite on-site hotels

Hard Rock Hotel

The Hard Rock Hotel at Universal Orlando Resort offers guests a rock star experience with its California-inspired architecture, rooms designed with contemporary decor and rock n' roll touches, and music memorabilia throughout the property. The hotel features a 12,000-square-foot pool with underwater speakers and a sandy beach area. Dining options include The Palm restaurant, The Kitchen restaurant, Emack & Bolio's Marketplace, and the Beach Club serving poolside fare. Guests enjoy exclusive theme park benefits including Universal Express Unlimited, Early Park Admission, and complimentary water taxis to Universal CityWalk.

Address:

Hard Rock Hotel Orlando
5800 Universal Blvd

What I love about Hard Rock Hotel:

The Hard Rock Hotel is just a short walk to the parks. The complimentary Express Pass benefits for guests allows you to skip regular lines at most popular attractions, essentially giving you more time to enjoy the parks. The lagoon-style pool with underwater music, sandy beaches, and slide creates a relaxing yet fun atmosphere after a day of adventure. I am still dreaming about the bacon, egg, and cheese croissants at Emack and Bolio's Marketplace, which is the perfect way to start the day.

Loews Royal Pacific Resort

Royal Pacific Resort is a luxury hotel featuring a South Pacific island theme with Polynesian-inspired architecture and tropical landscaping. The resort offers guests exclusive benefits including early park admission and complimentary Universal Express Unlimited passes for select attraction access. Dining options include Orchid Court Sushi Bar, Islands Dining Room, Tuk Tuk Marketplace, and the Bula Bar & Grille which serves tropical drinks by the lagoon-style pool. The hotel provides convenient boat transportation to Universal's theme parks and is within walking distance of Universal CityWalk.

Address:

Loews Royal Pacific Resort
6300 Hollywood Way
Orlando, FL 32819
phone: 1-407-503-3000

What I love about Loews Royal Pacific Resort:

The resort's South Pacific island theme creates a relaxing atmosphere. Universal Express Unlimited is included with your stay and allowing more time to experience the parks. The location is extremely convenient, with a picturesque walking path and water taxi service that takes you directly to the Universal parks and CityWalk. The sushi at Orchid Court Lounge is one of my favorite meals in the resort. For families with children, the supervised kids' club activities and character dining experiences add extra magic to the stay.

Universal's Cabana Bay Beach Resort

Cabana Bay Beach Resort is a vibrant, retro-themed hotel featuring mid-century modern design inspired by classic Florida beach resorts of the 1950s and 60s. This family-friendly resort offers affordable accommodations including standard rooms and family suites. Amenities include two large swimming pools, a lazy river, a waterslide, a bowling alley, fitness center, food court, and themed pool bars. The resort provides early park admission and convenient transportation to the three of the theme parks and Universal CityWalk. It is a popular choice for families seeking value without sacrificing comfort.

Address:

Universal's Cabana Bay Beach Resort
6550 Adventure Way,
Orlando, FL 32819
phone: 1-407-503-4000

What I love about Cabana Bay:

I absolutely love the view of and private entrance to Volcano Bay water park. The location allows for an easy walk to the parks or a quick shuttle ride. The two pool areas offer wonderful relaxation options, with the lazy river at the Cabana Courtyard being a personal favorite way to unwind. The resort's affordability makes it an excellent value compared to other Universal Orlando hotels, while still providing early park admission benefits.
