Why Universal Orlando Resort?

Adventure for Families

Universal Orlando Resort is a thrilling destination that offers extraordinary adventures for families of all ages. The resort features three distinct theme parks, each with unique attractions that appeal to different interests and age groups.
At Universal Studios Florida, families can step into the worlds of their favorite movies and TV shows. The Wizarding World of Harry Potter - Diagon Alley lets you explore iconic locations from the beloved series, while Transformers: The Ride 3D and Despicable Me Minion Mayhem offer high-tech interactive experiences.
Islands of Adventure delivers more intense thrills with Marvel Super Hero Island and Jurassic Park attractions. The Wizarding World of Harry Potter - Hogsmeade connects to Diagon Alley via the Hogwarts Express, creating an immersive magical experience across both parks.
Volcano Bay water theme park provides a perfect way to cool off with innovative water slides, a relaxing lazy river, and a massive wave pool beneath the towering volcano, Krakatau.
Beyond the parks, CityWalk offers family-friendly dining, shopping, and entertainment options.
The resort hotels cater to different budgets and preferences, with themed accommodations like the retro Cabana Bay Beach Resort and the Italian-inspired Loews Portofino Bay Hotel.
What makes Universal Orlando particularly special for families is its ability to entertain across generations. While there are gentle attractions for younger children, there are also pulse-pounding roller coasters and sophisticated entertainment for teens and adults. Interactive elements throughout the parks, like wands that cast spells in the Wizarding World, create magical moments for the whole family to enjoy together. Whether you're seeking thrilling rides, immersive themed environments, or refreshing water attractions, Universal Orlando Resort delivers a comprehensive family adventure that creates lasting memories.

Why Universal Orlando Resort?

Adventure for Adults

Universal Orlando Resort offers an exceptional experience tailored for adults seeking thrills, sophistication, and entertainment.
The parks transform into a playground for grown-ups with high-intensity attractions like the Jurassic World VelociCoaster and Hollywood Rip Ride Rockit coaster, which feature inversions and speeds that might be too intense for younger visitors. Hagrid's Magical Creatures Motorbike Adventure combines storytelling with adrenaline-pumping moments that adults particularly appreciate. Universal's immersive themed areas shine differently through an adult lens.
The Wizarding World of Harry Potter appeals to the generation that grew up with the books, offering authentic butterbeer and specialty cocktails at the Hog's Head pub. Springfield, home of The Simpsons, features adult humor and Moe's Tavern where you can enjoy a Duff beer. CityWalk transforms after dark with sophisticated dining options like Toothsome Chocolate Emporium & Savory Feast Kitchen and NBC Sports Grill & Brew, plus entertainment venues including Rising Star (karaoke with a live band) and nightclubs that come alive in the evening hours.
The resort's premier hotels offer adult-oriented amenities worth experiencing. Strong Water Tavern at Loews Sapphire Falls Resort features over 75 rums, while the Pool Bars at Hard Rock Hotel host occasional live music. Many hotels feature luxurious spas perfect for post-park relaxation. Adults can maximize their experience with Express Passes to bypass regular lines and take advantage of the "single rider" options available at most major attractions to significantly reduce wait times.
Whether you're planning a couples getaway, a friends trip, or solo adventure, Universal Orlando provides a perfect blend of nostalgic references, adult beverages, sophisticated dining, and heart-pounding thrills designed to satisfy the most discerning grown-up theme park enthusiasts.

Favorite on-site hotels

Hard Rock Hotel

The Hard Rock Hotel at Universal Orlando Resort offers guests a rock star experience with its California-inspired architecture and music memorabilia throughout the property. Located within walking distance of Universal's theme parks, the hotel features a 12,000-square-foot pool with underwater speakers and a sandy beach area. Accommodations range from standard rooms to deluxe suites, all designed with contemporary decor and rock n' roll touches. Dining options include The Kitchen restaurant, Emack & Bolio's Marketplace, and the Beach Club serving poolside fare. Guests enjoy exclusive theme park benefits including Universal Express Unlimited, Early Park Admission and complimentary water taxis to Universal CityWalk.

What I love about Hard Rock Hotel!

I find the Hard Rock Hotel to be particularly appealing for its close proximity to the parks. The complimentary Express Pass benefits for guests allows you to skip regular lines at most popular attractions, essentially giving you more time to enjoy the parks. The lagoon-style pool with underwater music, sandy beaches, and slide creates a relaxing yet fun atmosphere after a day of adventure. I am still dreaming about the bacon, egg, and cheese croissants at Emack and Bolio's, which is the perfect way to start the day. The rooms strike that perfect balance of comfort and style, with many offering views of the expansive pool area or the theme parks in the distance.

Loews Royal Pacific Resort

Lowe's Royal Pacific Resort is a luxury hotel located at Universal Orlando Resort in Florida. It features a South Pacific island theme with tropical landscaping, lush gardens, and Polynesian-inspired architecture. The resort offers guests exclusive benefits including early park admission and complimentary Universal Express Unlimited passes for attraction access. Dining options include Orchid Court Sushi Bar, Islands Dining Room, and the Bula Bar & Grille which serves tropical drinks by the lagoon-style pool. The hotel provides convenient boat transportation to Universal's theme parks and is within walking distance of Universal CityWalk.

What I love about Loews Royal Pacific Resort!

The resort's South Pacific island theme creates a relaxing tropical atmosphere with lush landscaping and Polynesian-inspired architecture. One of the biggest advantages is the included Universal Express Unlimited benefit, allowing hotel guests to skip regular lines at most attractions in Universal Studios and Islands of Adventure. The location is extremely convenient, with a picturesque walking path and water taxi service that takes you directly to the Universal parks and CityWalk entertainment district. The lagoon-style pool with its white sand beach offers a perfect place to unwind after a day at the parks, while the on-site dining options like Islands Dining Room and Orchid Court Lounge provide diverse culinary experiences with Pacific Rim influences. For families with children, the supervised kids' club activities and character dining experiences add extra magic to the stay.
